More Resources

If you didn't find the book you were looking for or it's unavailable, try the following resources:

Tufts Reserve allows professors or faculty to request the library to put aside a textbook or physical item behind the main desk, where students can check them out to use for short periods of time.

Tufts Interlibrary Loan enables Tufts students, professors, and staff to borrow books, CDs, DVDs, microfilm, and other materials from libraries worldwide.

Abebooks is an e-commerce global online marketplace that offer books, fine art, and collectables from sellers in over 50 countries.



Chegg provides digital and physical textbook rentals, textbooks, online tutoring, and other student services.

Slug Books is the easiest way to compare textbook prices so that you can buy or rent books cheap.

ECampus online retailer of new, used and electronic textbooks for sale or rent, study materials and book buyback services.



eBooks sells ebooks direct to consumers around the world that can be accessed online or downloaded to a vast array of devices.

Tufts Bookstore price matches if students find better prices on different websites to avoid all the extra fees.
