Book it Forward: Lending Library

Who runs this?

The FIRST Resource Center serves and creates a community of support to develop a network of resources for Tufts' first generation, low-income, and undocumented populations.

What is it?

One of these resources is the Book it Forward Lending Library where students can donate and access Tufts course required readings.

Who qualifies?

Students receiving financial aid with a Family Contribution of $10,000 and below may access books.
